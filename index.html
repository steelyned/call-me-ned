Process Approach

Human Resource Management

Understanding the most important resource

Recognizing the paramount importance of human resources, Human Resource Management (HRM or HR) represents a strategic approach to efficiently and effectively managing people within a company or organization. By leveraging HRM practices, businesses can gain a competitive advantage. This comprehensive approach is specifically designed to optimize employee performance in alignment with the employer's strategic objectives.

Supply Chain Management

A mutually beneficial relationship

Fostering a mutually beneficial relationship between vendors and customers is paramount to sustaining a seamless flow of materials or inputs that fulfill the manufacturing needs. This symbiotic connection guarantees the assurance of timely delivery and shorter lead times, contributing to operational efficiency. By actively cultivating this relationship, we ensure a reliable and consistent supply chain that meets your production requirements, thereby enhancing overall productivity and customer satisfaction.

Applications Programming

RDBMS & Web development

By implementing an RDBMS, we ensure that all critical information is stored and managed efficiently, enabling quick retrieval and analysis.

About me

Quality Management Systems (QMS) Practitioner | Consultant | Web Developer

My journey as a QMS practitioner began in the late 1990s, where I had the opportunity to manage, control, and guide our organization in designing, developing, and implementing its QMS. In my role as Management Representative (MR), Internal Auditor, and Documentation Specialist, I have worn many hats and gained extensive experience in various areas, including Training, Customer Relationship Management, Business Development, Human Resources Management, Quality Assurance, Web Development & Information Technology, and Design and Development.

As a consultant, I have also had the privilege of guiding and developing two other companies in their quest for ISO 9001 QMS certification. Throughout the years, I have been committed to delivering high-quality services and ensuring customer satisfaction by putting their needs at the forefront of all operations.

I retired in mid-2018 and decided to explore new opportunities. I found myself teaching English to Chinese students of various ages, which has been a fulfilling and rewarding experience. I have developed a strong bond with my students and their mothers, learning Mandarin to better communicate with them. In recognition of my efforts, I was awarded the Best 5-star teacher for the years 2020 and 2021. I have successfully passed the TESOL certification.

Although my career has taken a different direction, my previous experience in QMS has given me valuable skills and insights that I continue to apply to my teaching profession.
